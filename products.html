Our Coffee Collection

Gulp Roast Original Blend

"A masterfully balanced blend of the world’s finest beans." Our Original Blend is a carefully curated selection of premium Arabica and Robusta beans, sourced from the high-altitude farms of South America and Africa. Expertly roasted to perfection, this signature blend delivers a smooth, rich, and well-balanced flavor profile with notes of dark chocolate, toasted nuts, and a hint of caramel sweetness. Perfect for morning brews, drip coffee, or French press, this versatile blend offers a bold yet comforting cup that coffee lovers return to time and time again.

Gulp Roast Espresso

"Deep, bold, and crafted for the perfect shot." Made from hand-selected Arabica beans with a touch of high-quality Robusta, our Espresso Blend is roasted to an intense, full-bodied perfection. The result? A rich crema, deep cocoa undertones, and a smooth, velvety finish that pairs beautifully with milk-based drinks like lattes and cappuccinos. Whether you prefer a classic espresso shot, a strong Americano, or a luxurious flat white, this blend delivers an indulgent experience with every sip.

Gulp Roast Decaf

"Pure coffee experience, without the caffeine." Enjoy the full aroma and deep flavors of premium coffee—without the caffeine. Our Decaf Blend is crafted using the Swiss Water Process, a 100% chemical-free method that preserves the beans’ natural flavors and rich body. This decaf option maintains a beautifully balanced profile with smooth caramel notes, a hint of citrus brightness, and a nutty finish—making it the perfect choice for an evening cup or anyone who loves the taste of coffee without the caffeine kick.
